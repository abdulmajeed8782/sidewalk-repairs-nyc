Our Story

Eden Contracting NY has been serving the New York City area with professional sidewalk replacement and concrete services for years. We understand the unique challenges that property owners face when dealing with sidewalk violations, concrete repairs, and the demanding requirements of NYC's Department of Transportation.

Our team of licensed and insured professionals is dedicated to providing durable solutions for all your paving needs. From the bustling streets of Manhattan to the residential neighborhoods of Queens, Brooklyn, Bronx, and beyond, we have the expertise and equipment to handle projects of any size.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional sidewalk replacement and concrete services that ensure safety, compliance, and enhance the value of your property. We are committed to delivering superior quality workmanship while maintaining the highest standards of customer service.
